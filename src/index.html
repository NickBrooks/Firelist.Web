A Messenger chatbot for daily errands.

A minimalist and natural way to stay on top of your daily tasks using the chat service you already use.

Get started

.

Add daily tasks

Add tasks to today's list by sending Firelist the task. As you complete each task, simple mark it completed.

Pick up pasta sauce.

Get reminder notifications

Reminds are intuitive to set, just state the task and add a date and time and you'll get a notification at that time.

Cook pasta tomorrow at 6.30pm.

Up to 365 days

Each day has it's own Firelist, so you can add tasks and reminders to future lists simply by providing a date in the future.

Throw out leftover pasta in 5 days.
